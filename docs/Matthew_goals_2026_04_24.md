# The Semantic Climate Academy AI bot 

## Accessible systems perspective on Climate Change 

The bot will ideally be :

* able to answer questions about sustainability that are framed by
  - the CUTx Index
  - the proximity and dangers of various planetary boundaries
  - the fact that the atmosphere and the remaining natural resources should be shared equally by all people
  - the fact that the Earth is a Closed Mass System, is subject to the 2nd Law of Thermodynamics, and therefore all resources are finite (apart from diffuse solar energy)

* able to answer questions using the content of
  - IPCC AR, IPBES, UN Emissions Gap Report
  - peer reviewed papers from selected journals (Science, Nature, PNAS,...)

* able to reconcile possible conflicts between the CUTx Index framing and scientific publications (IPCC etc)

* able to answer in readable English (or other languages) that
  - does not require a degree in a scientific subject
  - assumes an age of 16 years upwards
  - does not crop out important qualifications of scientific technical detail

* able to point at which articles / sources it got its answer from.

* able to connect the levels of greenhouse gas concentrations in the atmosphere to levels of global warming

  - (inc. dry land and sea surface temperatures)  



* able to connect the remaining carbon budget and the consequent warming to the risk of destabilising Earth system components (circulation, biosphere, cryosphere) 


* be attentive to the potential of Earth systems to go over tipping points
   - tipping points = irreversible on human timescales, abrupt, lead to cascading effects
   = undermining of human civilisation on vast scale
   = environmental disasters will impact
     o Global South more heavily than Global North
     o vulnerable more than the wealthy in any nation
     o women more than men
     o young more than old

* be aware that there are different layers of probability embedded in our future emission pathways
   - it can be emphatic were needed, tentative when needed, and admit areas of understanding that are little understood

* be aware that there are different probabilities involved in our understanding of the different Earth Systems

* be aware that all NDCs and targets published by countries are not plugged into the remaining carbon budget

   - and should use the CUTx index to measure the relative truth of any political promise or statement about that countries commitment to emissions/resource extraction reductions. 



* be answers that (where relevant) are informed by the precautionary principle
